Registration
THE REGISTRATION PROCESS FOR GRAMMAR FEST’16 IS AS FOLLOWS:
  1. Fill in your team's details in the form below. Make sure that you write down all the required details, and that no important information is left out.
  2. Once you press the register button, a 'thank you' message should appear on your screen. This means that your registration form has been submitted successfully.
  3. The head delegate will receive a confirmation email from the registration team here at Grammar Fest.


Points to remember:
  • Each delegation can only have ONE head delegate.
  • The deadline for submitting the form is 10th September.
  • The delegation fee and the participation fee for all delegates must be paid at LGS Paragon. The school office will be open for delegates to visit once classes resume from 15th August, between the hours of 8 a.m and 3 p.m.
* The head delegate must be in contact with the registration team at the time of payment.
